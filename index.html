About Us

Alysha, Anthony, and Chris are fourth year students at Simon Fraser University. Alysha is working on completing a Computer Science Degree, Spatial Information System Certificate, and has already completed the Co-op Education Designation. Anthony and Chris are both working on completing a Geography Degree, Spatial Information Systems Certificate, and have completed the Co-op Education Designation. Together, these 3 students make up team mapit.space.

Alysha van Duynhoven
Lead Developer, GoElectric Web Application

Alysha has had several internships which have provided her with diverse experiences in front-end and back-end software development. Alysha loves programming, GIScience, and technology. When away from her computer, she enjoys hiking, kayaking, stargazing, photography, playing soccer, and creating things.

Chris Yee
Project Lead & Designer, GoElectric Interface Design, Logos, and Homepage

Chris is currently a GIS Technician at the Fire Underwriters Survey and has previously worked with the Department of Fisheries and Oceans as a GIS Coordinator. Outside of academics and work, Chris spends his time snowboarding, mountain biking, and practicing photography.
